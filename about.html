About Prime Guard Solutions

Professional SIA-Licensed Security Services Across the United Kingdom

Our Company

Prime Guard Solutions is a professional security services provider operating across the United Kingdom. We specialise in providing SIA-licensed personnel and tailored security solutions for businesses and organisations of all sizes.

Our approach is grounded in professionalism, regulatory compliance, and honest communication. We do not make exaggerated claims or unverified guarantees. Instead, we focus on delivering reliable, compliant security services through trained personnel and proven operational practices.

Commitment to Professionalism

SIA-Licensed Personnel

All security officers employed by Prime Guard Solutions hold valid Security Industry Authority (SIA) licences, meeting mandatory UK regulatory requirements. Our personnel undergo regular training and professional development to maintain the highest standards of conduct and competence.

Industry Standards & Training

We invest in continuous professional development and training for our team. Our security personnel are trained in industry best practices, customer service, conflict management, and compliance procedures relevant to their operational roles.

Our Professional Approach

Regulatory Compliance

Full compliance with UK security regulations, the Security Industry Authority (SIA) framework, GDPR data protection requirements, and industry best practices in all operations.

UK-Focused Expertise

Operating exclusively across the United Kingdom with deep understanding of local compliance requirements, regional regulations, and industry standards specific to UK operations.

Honest Communication

We communicate honestly about security capabilities, service delivery, and limitations. We avoid making unverified claims or unrealistic guarantees about crime prevention or security outcomes.

Tailored Solutions

Each client receives security services tailored to their specific requirements, venue characteristics, and operational needs—not generic, one-size-fits-all approaches.
