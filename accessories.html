Security Accessories

Information about professional security equipment and accessories

Prime Guard Solutions provides information about professional security accessories used in industry. These items support our security services delivery and professional operations. This page is informational only—we are not a retailer of security accessories.

Communication Equipment

Two-Way Radios

Professional communication equipment enabling real-time coordination between security personnel across sites. Two-way radios allow instant communication for incident response and operational management.

Radio Earpieces & Headsets

Discreet communication accessories allowing security personnel to maintain hands-free communication while performing duties. Enables professional appearance while maintaining operational communication.

Mobile Communication Systems

Mobile phone and data communication systems used for reporting, coordination, and incident documentation. Enables off-site management and emergency communication capabilities.

Uniforms & Safety Gear

Security Uniforms

Professional security uniforms including shirts, trousers, and jackets identifying personnel as security officers. Uniforms convey professional authority and identify personnel to clients and visitors.

Badges & ID Credentials

Professional identification badges and credentials identifying personnel by name and role. Credentials establish credibility and enable identification verification.

Safety & Protective Equipment

Safety vests, hard hats, and protective equipment used in operational environments. Safety equipment protects personnel and ensures compliance with workplace safety regulations.

Tactical Gear & Equipment

Professional equipment including belts, holsters, and utility gear for security operations. Tactical gear provides professional appearance and operational functionality.

Access Control Equipment

Access Control Systems

Electronic systems controlling facility access using keycards, fobs, or biometric identification. Access control manages who enters restricted areas and maintains audit records.

ID Card Systems

Equipment for issuing identification cards and credentials to authorised personnel and visitors. ID systems establish credibility and enable verification of access authorisation.

Visitor Management Systems

Check-in systems and visitor logs documenting who enters and exits secured facilities. Visitor systems create audit trails and manage temporary access permissions.

Gate & Door Security Hardware

Gatehouse hardware, doors, intercoms, and barrier systems controlling entry points. Hardware enables physical security enforcement and access management.

Crowd Control Barriers & Equipment

Queue Barriers

Retractable queue barriers controlling flow and managing waiting lines. Barriers organise crowds and direct movement in predictable patterns.

Crowd Control Barriers

Modular fencing and barrier systems managing crowd movement and restricting access. Barriers create physical separation and direct pedestrian traffic flow.

Signage & Directional Equipment

Professional signage directing crowds, indicating restricted areas, and providing safety information. Signage communicates instructions and maintains crowd control.

CCTV & Surveillance Accessories

CCTV Cameras

Professional surveillance cameras for monitoring facilities. CCTV provides visual evidence documentation and acts as deterrent to unauthorised activity.

Recording & Storage Systems

Digital video recording systems storing surveillance footage. Recording systems create permanent audit records and evidence documentation.

Monitor & Display Systems

Control centre monitors and display systems for real-time surveillance viewing. Monitors enable active monitoring and rapid incident response.

Mounting & Infrastructure

Camera mounting hardware, cabling, and infrastructure supporting surveillance systems. Infrastructure enables proper camera placement and signal transmission.
